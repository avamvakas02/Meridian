The word meridian represents a point of highest focus and alignment. That’s what Meridian is built to deliver: a clean, intuitive task management experience that helps you prioritize what matters and keep real momentum.

Designed for Focus

Meridian is thoughtfully crafted for professionals managing complex workloads, students organizing responsibilities, teams collaborating on shared goals, and individuals building productive habits. No unnecessary features. No distractions. Just streamlined task management.

Under the Hood
